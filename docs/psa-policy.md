# Cariboo Signals — PSA & Partner Organization Policy

*Effective May 2026*

---

## What PSAs Are

Each Cariboo Signals episode includes a community spotlight segment featuring a public service announcement (PSA) for a local organization. PSAs are short, factual mentions of the organization's name, mandate, and contact information, drawn from publicly available sources. They are not paid placements and do not constitute endorsement.

---

## How Organizations Are Selected

Organizations are selected for the PSA rotation based on the following criteria:

- Located in or directly serving the Cariboo-Chilcotin region of BC
- Non-profit, community-serving, or public-benefit mandate
- Publicly listed contact information available
- Relevance to one or more of the show's weekly themes

The current rotation includes approximately 38 organizations. The full list is maintained in [`config/psa_organizations.json`](../config/psa_organizations.json) in the project repository.

---

## Notification

Organizations added to the rotation prior to May 2026 were selected from public sources and were not individually notified before their first appearance. We are in the process of contacting listed organizations to confirm they are comfortable being featured.

Organizations added from May 2026 onward are notified by email before their first appearance and given the opportunity to decline or request changes to how they are described.

---

## Opting Out or Requesting Changes

Any organization featured in a Cariboo Signals PSA may request:

- **Removal** from the rotation
- **Changes** to how they are described
- **Confirmation** of the language used before future appearances

To make any of these requests, email **feedback@cariboosignals.ca** with the subject line "PSA: [Organization Name]". Requests are processed within 5 business days. Removal requests are permanent and will be honoured without question.

---

## What PSAs Are Not

- PSAs are not paid sponsorships or advertising
- Being featured does not imply a formal partnership or endorsement relationship
- Organizations do not have editorial control over episode content beyond their own PSA description
